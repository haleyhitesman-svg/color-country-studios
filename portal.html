QuickBooks Integration

Connect your QuickBooks Online account to enable live invoice syncing for parents.

Step 1 — Create Your App on Intuit Developer

You only need to do this once. It takes about 10 minutes and gives your website permission to read invoices from your QuickBooks account.

  1. 1
    Go to developer.intuit.com and sign in with your QuickBooks credentials.
  2. 2
    Click "My Apps" → "Create an App" → select "QuickBooks Online and Payments".
  3. 3
    Name it "Color Country Studios Portal" and select all scopes including Accounting.
  4. 4
    Go to Development Settings → Keys & OAuth. Copy your Client ID and Client Secret.

Step 2 — Add Your Credentials to Your .env File

On your web hosting server, create a file called .env in your website's root folder and add these lines. Never share this file publicly.

# QuickBooks Online API Credentials # Get these from developer.intuit.com → My Apps → Keys & OAuth QB_CLIENT_ID=YOUR_CLIENT_ID_HERE QB_CLIENT_SECRET=YOUR_CLIENT_SECRET_HERE QB_REDIRECT_URI=https://colorcountrycloggers.com/auth/callback QB_ENVIRONMENT=production # Stripe (for payment processing) STRIPE_PUBLISHABLE_KEY=YOUR_STRIPE_PUBLIC_KEY STRIPE_SECRET_KEY=YOUR_STRIPE_SECRET_KEY # Database (Supabase) SUPABASE_URL=https://lwfgwemmyedehztlnypj.supabase.co SUPABASE_ANON_KEY=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9...YOUR_KEY

Step 3 — Authorize the Connection

Once your credentials are saved, click the button below to authorize Color Country Studios Portal to connect to your QuickBooks account. You'll be redirected to QuickBooks to approve access.

Not yet connected — add your credentials above first

What Happens After Connection

Once connected, the portal will automatically:

  • ✅ Pull all parent customers and their invoices from QuickBooks every 15 minutes
  • ✅ Show each parent only their own invoices and balance when they log in
  • ✅ Display overdue amounts with red alerts
  • ✅ Generate secure Stripe payment links for each invoice
  • ✅ Mark invoices as paid in QuickBooks automatically when payment is received
  • ✅ Send email receipts to parents after payment
