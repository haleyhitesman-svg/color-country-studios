Our Story

Two Studios,
One Legacy

"What started with a dream and a small backyard studio has grown into something truly extraordinary."

In 1976, Color Country Studios was born in Parowan, Utah. Over 50 years, our program has grown into a nationally ranked home for dance, training hundreds of dedicated performers across two specialized studios.

Color Country Cloggers is our nationally recognized clogging program — competitive, high-energy, and rooted in American tradition. Sunshine Dance brings the joy of ballet, jazz, lyrical, and tumbling to all ages in a warm, encouraging environment.

Under the direction of Heather Mineer and our passionate team of instructors, every dancer finds their stage.

★
Company

Clogging Teams (Mini, Junior, Elementary, Senior) are full competition teams — each competing at 2 competitions with 2 dances. Dance Teams have select competition opportunities depending on the team. All teams perform locally, represent our studio with pride, and participate in our studio recitals every December and April.
