Our Story

Two Studios,
One Legacy

"What started with a dream and a small backyard studio has grown into something truly extraordinary."

In 1976, Color Country Studios was born in Parowan, Utah. Over 50 years, our program has grown into a nationally ranked home for dance, training hundreds of dedicated performers across two specialized studios.

Color Country Cloggers is our nationally recognized clogging program with 7 teams ranging from Novice Stars to Advanced Gold Elite and our specialty Irish teams. Sunshine Dance offers ballet, jazz, lyrical, and tumbling with 7 dance teams from Preschool through our Elite Senior Company.

Under the direction of Heather Mineer and our passionate team of instructors, every dancer finds their stage.

Clogging

Our signature discipline. Rhythmic, energetic, and deeply rooted in American tradition — the art that put us on the national map.

The foundation of all dance. We develop strength, grace, and proper technique in a nurturing environment for all ages.

High-energy, expressive movement that builds performance confidence, musicality, and stage presence.

🌊
Lyrical

The beautiful blend of ballet and jazz, emphasizing emotional expression and fluid storytelling through movement.

Tumbling

Three progressive levels build strength, flexibility, and aerial skills from foundational rolls to advanced acrobatics.
